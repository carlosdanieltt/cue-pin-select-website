Your passwords, derived but never stored.

Cue Vault generates strong, unique passwords from a PIN, passphrase, and a 4-letter cue. No vault to breach. No cloud to compromise. Works entirely offline.

Biometric Auth

Unlock with Face ID or Touch ID. Your master credentials stay behind biometric security.

Works Offline

No internet. No servers. No accounts. Everything runs locally on your device.

Derivable by Hand

Dark Mode

Search & Organize

How It Works

1

Set Up Your Vault

Choose a 4-digit PIN and a memorable passphrase. These are your only secrets.

2

Add Your Services

For each account, enter a name and a 4-letter cue. A strong, unique password is generated instantly.

3

Retrieve Anytime

Authenticate with Face ID, tap a service, and copy the password. Or derive it by hand with no app needed.
