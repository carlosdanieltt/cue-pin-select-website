Overview

This privacy policy describes how your personal information is handled in the Cue Vault app.

We do not collect, use, save, or have access to any of your personal data through the Cue Vault app. Individual settings relating to the Cue Vault app are not personal and are stored only on your device.

The Cue Vault app does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.
