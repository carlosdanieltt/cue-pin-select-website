Privacy Policy

Overview

This privacy policy describes how your personal information is handled in the Cue Pin Select app.

We collect no personal information using the Cue Pin Select app

We do not collect, use, save, or have access to any of your personal data through the Cue Pin Select app. Individual settings relating to the Cue Pin Select app are not personal and are stored only on your device.

The Cue Pin Select app complies with the children's online privacy protection act

The Cue Pin Select app does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.
